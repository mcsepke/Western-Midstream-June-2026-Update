Worth discussing over lunch FOR A MIDSTREAM OPERATOR

Questions to anchor the conversation

  1. Identity is the new perimeter. If an attacker compromised your Active Directory or Entra tenant tonight, how fast could you see what changed and surgically revert it — without restoring the whole forest?
  2. Clean recovery, not just recovery. When you fail over after an incident, how do you prove the recovery point itself isn't carrying the malware? (Threat Hunting + Anomaly Detection feed directly into orchestrated, isolated recovery.)
  3. One pane across on-prem and cloud. As workloads move to Azure, AWS and M365, is your backup and threat coverage following them — or fragmenting into separate tools?
  4. SaaS identity & data. Okta, Entra and M365 now hold the keys to operations. Are those backed up and revertible the same way your AD forest is?
